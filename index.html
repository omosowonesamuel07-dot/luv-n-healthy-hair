Passion for Healthy Hair
✦ Certified Hair Loss Practitioner

I have been a licensed cosmetologist for 34 years, graduating from Cope Vocational Center at 18 — and I've been working with passion ever since. My love for hair started as a little girl, playing with my dolls and styling my mother's hair. It was always more than just a hobby; it was a calling.

From the very beginning of my career, I invested in myself — taking training classes, color classes, cutting classes, business classes — and I am proud to be a certified hair loss practitioner. I've always implemented everything I've learned, because my clients deserve nothing less than the best.

My goal has always been to build a clientele with strong, healthy hair. I believe the foundation must be strong — it all begins with the follicle. Understanding the science of hair creates that strong foundation, and healthy hair naturally follows.

Above all, I love people. I understand that communicating and truly listening to each client creates a relationship built on trust — knowing their likes, their dislikes, their goals. Taking a personal interest in each individual and giving them quality time and service brings me an unexplainable joy that keeps me going every single day.
